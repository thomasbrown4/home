How to play

Hockey is a contact team sport that is played on ice. There are 6 players on each team (1 goalie, 3 forwards and 2 defenceman). Hockey is quite popular in Canada and although many people think it is Canada's national sport it is not. The NHL(National Hockey Leauge)is the highest level of hockey for men and is also the strongest pro hockey leauge. The objective of the game is to get the puck in the other teams net using your stick.

Rules of hockey

The rules are quite simple. Score more goals than the other team by the end of the 3 periods, players cant use a skate to kick the puck in or throw the puck in the net intentionally. Each team has 5 skaters, 3 forwards 2 defencemen and a goalie. Professional NHL games are consisted of three 20 minute periods if the score is tied after the 3 periods, a 5 minute overtime is needed, if the score is still tied then a shootout will be needed to determine a winner. There are also several other rules like the offside and the icing. Offside: When the player crosses the other teams blue line before the puck does. Icing: when a team shoots the puck before the center red line crossing the opposing teams goal line.
